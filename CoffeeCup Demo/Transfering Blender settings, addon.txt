Transferring Blender settings, addons, and hotkeys from one computer to another

	
	1.search the following location on your Windows pc:


		%appdata%\Blender Foundation\Blender\

	2. You'll see a folder labelled "4.5" or whatever edition you're using.
	
	3. Copy and paste this folder from the old computer to the new computer in the same location

	4. Startup a blender file in the new computer and ensure the addons, etc. are enabled.
